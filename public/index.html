How To Play:

  • Drawers are randomly selected, before the game begins!
  • The Drawer is randomly assigned a card to draw
  • All other players will lock in their answers when they think they know what is being drawn
  • Points are given for correct answers
  • Each round, a new drawer is randomly choosen!
